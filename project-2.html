**Bangladesh In Our Motherland**

It is my privilege to warmly welcome you to the Bangladesh-Korea Technical Training Center (BKTTC), Chittagong. It is a great institution dedicated to the cause of quality Technical and Vocational Education & Training programs both for local & overseas employment. Having its door opened in 1962, this training center has established its reputation as a leader in the field of Technical and Vocational Education & Training (TVET). In the year 2013 Korea International Co-operation Agency (KOICA) has remodeled this center and equipped with international level of training facilities with a view to cater the items of new skill development policy so that it can continue its leadership in TVET for future days.The center is remodeled by the mission of providing quality TVET program (i.e) Competency Based & Assessment (CBT&A),that will prepare competent graduates in different occupation for the world of work with the capacity to contribute to national development. This center is going to be pioneer in CBT&A in Bangladesh with the most comprehensive training package and other first class facilities. Korean Government has created this opportunities through KOICA. We are grateful to them and appreciate their effort & cooperation. We offer a wide range of technical & professional programs for school lavers, TVET trainer, technicians and those seeking new carrier paths.
